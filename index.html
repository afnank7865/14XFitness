Our Facilities

InBody Test


An InBody test is a body composition analysis that uses bioelectrical impedance analysis (BIA) to assess the proportions of fat, muscle, and water in the body.

Cardiorespriatory Endurance Training

Cardiovascular endurance training, also known as aerobic training, focuses on improving the efficiency of your heart, lungs, and circulatory system.

Cross-Fit & Functional Training

Cross-Fit & Functional Training are intense workouts that boost strength, endurance, and agility through varied, real-life movement exercises.

Strength Training

Strength Training builds muscle, boosts endurance, and increases overall power using weights, resistance, or bodyweight exercises.

Free Weight Training

Free Weight Training uses dumbbells, barbells, and kettlebells to build muscle, strength, and balance through controlled, full-range movements.

Power Training

Power Training focuses on explosive, high-intensity movements to build strength, speed, and overall athletic power and performance.

Core Training

Core Training targets the abdominal and lower back muscles to improve stability, balance, posture, and overall strength.

Kick-Boxing & MMA

Kickboxing & MMA are high-intensity combat sports combining striking, agility, and self-defense, enhancing strength, endurance, and coordination.
